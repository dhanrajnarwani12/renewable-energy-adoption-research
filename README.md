# Renewable Energy Adoption Research

## Research Topic
Socio-Economic and Personal Drivers of Renewable Energy Adoption: A Moderated Mediation Framework for Developing Countries

## Project Overview
This academic research project investigates the factors that influence renewable energy adoption intention among households in Pakistan.

The study is based on the Theory of Planned Behavior (TPB) and uses a moderated mediation framework to understand how personal, social, environmental, and economic factors affect renewable energy adoption.

## Research Objective
This research investigates the factors influencing renewable energy adoption intentions among households in Pakistan using the Theory of Planned Behavior (TPB).
